Hamburguer

A classic American dish featuring a seasoned beef patty, usually grilled or pan-fried, served in a bun with a variety of toppings like lettuce, tomato, cheese, and sauces. It's a beloved fast food item, easy to customize with your favorite fixings.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (or other meats like turkey or chicken)
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, and pickles (optional toppings)
  • Cheese (optional, like cheddar or American)
  • Onions (optional)
  • Ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise (optional sauces)
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Preparation

  1. Season the ground beef with salt and pepper, then shape it into patties.
  2. Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook the patties for 4-5 minutes per side, or until your desired level of doneness is reached.
  4. Toast the burger buns lightly on the grill or in a pan.
  5. Assemble the burger: place the cooked patty on the bottom bun, then add cheese, lettuce, tomato, pickles, and your preferred sauces.
  6. Top with the other half of the bun and serve.
