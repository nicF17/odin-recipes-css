Milanesa with french fries

A crispy breaded and fried meat cutlet, usually made with beef or chicken, served with golden, crispy French fries. It's a comforting and hearty meal with a perfect balance of crunch and tenderness.

Ingredients

  • Meat (usually beef or chicken, pounded thin)
  • Eggs (for dipping)
  • Bread crumbs (for coating)
  • All-purpose flour (for dredging)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • French fries (potatoes, cut and fried)
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)

Preparation

  1. Season the meat with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. Prepare three shallow bowls: one with flour, one with beaten eggs, and one with bread crumbs.
  3. Dredge the meat in flour, dip it in the egg, then coat it with bread crumbs.
  4. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Fry the breaded meat until golden and crispy on both sides (about 4-5 minutes per side, depending on thickness).
  6. For French fries: peel and cut the potatoes into strips. Fry them in hot oil until golden and crispy.
  7. Serve the Milanese with French fries on the side.
