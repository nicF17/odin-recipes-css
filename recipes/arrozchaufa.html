Arroz chaufa

A Peruvian-style fried rice, typically made with leftover rice stir-fried with vegetables, scrambled eggs, soy sauce, and your choice of protein like chicken, beef, or shrimp. It's a delicious, savory dish with a touch of Chinese culinary influence.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice (preferably cold or leftover)
  • Eggs (scrambled)
  • Soy sauce
  • Green onions (chopped)
  • Bell peppers (chopped)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Protein (chicken, beef, shrimp, or pork)
  • Vegetables (like peas, carrots, or corn)
  • Sesame oil (optional)
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Preparation

  1. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add chopped vegetables (onions, peppers, garlic) and cook until tender.
  3. Add your choice of protein (chicken, beef, shrimp, etc.) and cook until browned and cooked through.
  4. Push the ingredients to one side of the pan and scramble eggs on the other side until cooked.
  5. Add the cold rice and stir everything together, breaking up any clumps.
  6. Pour in soy sauce, and stir to coat the rice evenly.
  7. Season with salt, pepper, and sesame oil (optional).
  8. Garnish with green onions and serve hot.
